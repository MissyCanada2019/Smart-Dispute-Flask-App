Our Story: Built from the Trenches, For Your Fight

I didn’t build this app because I’m a developer or a lawyer. I built it because I had to.

I’ve lived through what too many Canadians are still living through right now:

  • Mold, infestations, and unsafe housing with no real help.
  • Landlords who ignore your basic rights because they think you won’t fight back.
  • Eviction threats while trying to raise a family on low income or ODSP.
  • Children with disabilities being denied proper accommodations.
  • Hours spent trying to understand legal forms written in language meant to confuse.
  • Doors slammed shut because you don’t have a lawyer or a few hundred dollars.

I’ve filed T2 and T6 applications, navigated the Landlord and Tenant Board system on my own, and stood up to housing providers who expected me to stay silent. I’ve written my own submissions, organized my own evidence, and stayed up nights building rebuttals — not just for me, but for others who had no one else.

I learned to read legal rules, tear apart false claims, and turn pain into action. But I never forgot how it felt to be alone in that fight.

That’s why I created Smart Dispute Canada — so no one has to go through that fight alone again.

This app isn’t about theory. It’s built from the trenches. It’s built for single moms, tenants, newcomers, people with disabilities, and anyone who’s ever been told “you have no chance.”

Smart Dispute Canada gives people real tools to fight back — in plain language, with real-time support, guided timelines, and legal documents you can actually understand and use.
